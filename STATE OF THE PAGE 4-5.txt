## Global architecture (not per-fold)

- **Navigation** is a global fixed overlay that lives above all folds — 
  individual fold components do not render or account for it.
- **Lenis** smooth scroll is installed at the page root. Every fold uses 
  `useScroll` from Framer Motion normally; Lenis enhances transparently, 
  no per-fold import needed.
- **Sticky mobile WhatsApp bar** is owned by Fold 1 and persists across 
  the entire page on mobile only (`lg:hidden`). Its visibility is driven 
  by IntersectionObserver on the hero ref — the bar appears when less 
  than 25% of the hero is in view, and disappears when the hero re-enters.
- **Root wrapper** enforces `overflow-x: clip` and `max-w-[100vw]` to 
  prevent mobile horizontal scroll — no fold may rely on its own section 
  clipping alone.
- **Image configuration convention**: each fold declares a top-level 
  `const FOLD_IMAGES = { desktop, mobile, alt }` block for easy swap when 
  real photography arrives.
- **Placeholder WhatsApp URL**: 
  `https://wa.me/5500000000000?text=Ol%C3%A1!%20Quero%20criar%20minha%20pr%C3%B3pria%20marca%20de%20vinhos%20com%20a%20Adega%20Pr%C3%B3pria.`

## CTA cadence across the page

Conversion moments are rare and earned. The page must not offer the CTA 
at every opportunity — that cheapens the transformation. Current rhythm:

- **Fold 1 (Hero)**: primary CTA "Crie Sua Marca Agora" — the invitation
- **Folds 2, 3**: NO CTA — emotional accumulation
- **Fold 4 (Interstitial)**: primary CTA "Crie Sua Marca Agora" — the demand
- **Fold 5**: NO CTA (likely) — emotional depth before proof
- **Folds 6, 8, 9**: CTAs return at the end of each (process → proof → finale)

When building Fold 5, resist the impulse to add a CTA. The pressure should 
build through the end of Fold 5 and release in Fold 6.

## System rhythm patterns (continuity signals)

**Container width progression** — width carries pacing signal:
- Hero (immersive): `max-w-5xl`
- Video (focused contained): `max-w-6xl` with `max-w-4xl` frame
- Manifesto (editorial expansive): `max-w-7xl`
- Interstitial (intimate demand): `max-w-5xl`
- Every new fold should choose its container width deliberately.

**Eyebrow style — three conventions now established:**
- **Interpuncts** (`WORD · WORD · WORD`) when stacking credibility signals — Fold 1
- **Em-dash flanks** (`— WORD —`) for single-word editorial quiet — Folds 2, 3
- **Vertical tick** (`│  WORD`) for conversion-oriented declarative — Fold 4
- New folds may introduce a fourth convention ONLY if the tonal shift 
  justifies it. Otherwise, pick one of the three.

**Gold italic words already used** (avoid repeating):
- "Finos" (Fold 1), "única" (Fold 2), "herança" + "marcas" (Fold 3), 
  "merece" (Fold 4)
- Every new headline needs its own distinct emphasis word — the emotional 
  pivot of the sentence, never a filler adjective.

**Headline weight logic:**
- Fraunces **700** when declaring aspiration (Fold 1 Hero)
- Fraunces **400** when the register is prose, meditation, or massive-scale 
  declaration where the type size carries the weight (Folds 2, 3, 4)
- Pick weight based on emotional register, not content importance.

**Trust signal evolution:**
- Fold 1: atmospheric reassurance ("Atendimento personalizado via WhatsApp")
- Fold 4: contractual commitment ("Resposta em até 2 horas · Segunda a Sábado")
- Future CTA folds should not copy Fold 4's exact phrasing — find their 
  own angle (testimonial tone, volume signal, etc.).

**CTA differentiation rule:**
- Every CTA across the page drives to the same WhatsApp URL and may 
  share the same text, BUT the pill's visual weight must escalate with 
  page depth. Fold 1's CTA = invitation (smaller, MessageCircle only). 
  Fold 4's CTA = demand (larger, MessageCircle + ArrowUpRight, hover 
  shimmer). Folds 6, 8, 9 should continue this escalation — the final 
  CTA in Fold 9 is the most visually weighted pill on the page.
